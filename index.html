About Me

I am currently a senior R&D computer vision engineer at Kitware Inc. Prior to working at Kitware Inc, I received my doctoral degree in computer science from The University of Texas at Dallas in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Feng Chen. In 2016, I received dual M.S. degrees in computer science and biomedical science from University at Albany, SUNY and Albany Medical College, respectively. My researches focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Mining, and Computer Vision.
